A quick update on this 2022 solstice,

I've uploaded all the documentation for the Rotary Un-Smartphone to the 
web page. I'm staunchly open source but had been waiting to make the 
information public until phones are shipping out to protect myself a bit 
from knockoff manufacturers. We're close enough now, it's fine.

To remind, here's the web page: 
https://skysedge.com/unsmartphones/RUSP/index.html

Direct links:

-Assembly Guide 
<https://docs.google.com/document/d/e/2PACX-1vRfhCvYx7I4NWDRWsKIRIkdUYgYihmH3PclluxLtJn0RdxL3zioXr2-NP-UySFU5GyZhOAWxyLWP9jA/pub>: 
It's a draft. I'm working on taking pictures, but the text is pretty 
much intact. It's a living document sourced from my Google docs right 
now but I'll publish it as a PDF once the pictures are there.

-Firmware <https://github.com/jhaupt/RotaryUnSmartphone>: The link 
points to GitHub. Lots of kluges in there. Don't look.

-Electrical <https://skysedge.com/unsmartphones/RUSP/electrical/>: All 
the KiCad project files for the Motherboard, Daughterboard, Faceplate, 
and Antenna Board.

-Mechanical <https://skysedge.com/unsmartphones/RUSP/mechanical/>: STEP 
files of every part, assemblies, and mechanical drawings of machined parts.

Happy holidays to everyone.

~Justine
