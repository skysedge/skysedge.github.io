PLA Filament

$29.00 $35.00

 

Available in 1 kg spools and a wide array of colors, our PLA (Polylactic Acid) filament measures 1.75 mm in diameter and is one of the easiest filament materials to print with. 

We recommend printing PLA at 215-220 °C with the bed heated to 60-70 °C depending on the surface preparation used. It is possible to print PLA directly on glass, but is much easier to maintain adhesion on polyimide film.

MakerGear PLA filament has been rigorously tested to ensure consistent, high quality performance.
